My 2009 Acura TSX

Performance Modifications

My Acura TSX 09 is equipped with several upgrades to enhance its performance:

  • AEM Cold Air Intake
  • Hondata Engine Management System
  • Skunk2 Headers
  • Yonaka Exhaust System

These modifications have significantly improved my car's performance, making it faster and more responsive.

Benefits of Upgrades

With these modifications, I have noticed:

  1. Increased horsepower
  2. Better throttle response
  3. Enhanced engine sound
  4. Improved fuel efficiency
